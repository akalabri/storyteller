### The Promise


Scene 1: The Morning on the Farm
The sun rose gently over the small farm, and Hamza hurried outside before breakfast. His favorite goat, Zaytoun, was already awake, nibbling on fresh grass.
“Good morning, Zaytoun!” Hamza laughed, patting her soft white head.

His father called from behind him, “Hamza, please make sure the small wooden gate is closed after you feed them.”
“I promise, Baba!” he replied confidently.

Scene 2: The Little Mistake
After feeding the goats, Hamza heard his friends calling him from the road. Excited, he ran to play — forgetting to check the gate.

An hour later, Baba’s voice echoed across the yard.
“Hamza! Zaytoun is not here!”

Hamza’s heart began to beat fast. He remembered the gate. It might have been left open.

Scene 3: The Choice
He could say nothing. Maybe Baba would think the wind pushed it open.
But his chest felt heavy.

Hamza walked slowly to his father.
“Baba… I forgot to close the gate. I’m sorry.”

There was silence for a moment. Then Baba placed his hand gently on Hamza’s shoulder.
“Thank you for telling the truth. Let’s go find Zaytoun together.”

Scene 4: The Right Place
They found Zaytoun near the olive trees at the edge of the farm, happily chewing leaves.

On the way back, Baba smiled.
“Everyone makes mistakes, Hamza. But honesty always brings you back to the right place.”

Hamza looked at Zaytoun and smiled.
His heart felt light again.
